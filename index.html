Services

Here are some of the services I offer:

Assist with data analysis

With my expertise in data analysis, I can assist with cleaning and organizing data, running statistical analyses, and creating basic graphs and charts. I'll work closely with you to ensure that your data is accurate and your analysis is sound.

Develop research methods and protocols

As an experienced epidemiologist, I can help you design and implement research studies that address your specific research questions or public health concerns. I will work closely with you to develop study protocols, identify appropriate research methods, and ensure that your research adheres to ethical and regulatory guidelines. Whether you're looking to conduct observational studies, clinical trials, or other types of research, I can help you every step of the way.

Implement infection control measures

As an epidemiologist, I understand the importance of implementing effective infection control measures to prevent the spread of disease. I can help you develop and implement strategies to reduce the risk of infection in a variety of settings, from hospitals and nursing homes to schools and workplaces. Using principles of infectious disease epidemiology, I can help you identify high-risk areas, implement appropriate protective measures, and monitor the effectiveness of your infection control program. With my help, you can keep your staff, patients, and community safe from infectious diseases.

Participate in field investigations

I'm experienced in conducting field investigations and have worked on outbreak investigations and other fieldwork in the past. I can help you collect data, conduct interviews, or assist with other tasks as needed to ensure your investigation is successful.

Draft reports and presentations

As your epidemiology consultant, I can help you create reports or presentations that effectively communicate the findings from your epidemiological studies or public health projects. I'll collaborate with you to craft clear and concise messaging that resonates with your target audience.
